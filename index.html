𝙿𝙷𝙾𝚃𝙾 𝙽𝙰𝙼𝙴 𝚃𝙸𝙼𝙴𝙻𝙸𝙽𝙴 𝙺𝙴𝚈 𝙰𝙲𝙲𝙾𝙼𝙿𝙻𝙸𝚂𝙷𝙼𝙴𝙽𝚃𝚂 𝙸𝚗𝚏𝚘:
Jose Rizal
  • Born: June 19, 1861
  • Died: December 30,1896 (35 yrs old)
  • Integration of the Philippines as a province of Spain
  • Representation in the Cortes ( AKA Spanish parliament)
  • Replacement of Spanish friars by Filipino priests
  • Freedom of assembly and expression
  • Equality of Filipinos and Spaniards before the law
A Filipino Writer and Polymath
Emilio Aguinaldo y Famy
  • Born: March 22, 1869
  • Died: February 6, 1964 (94 yrs old)
  • President of the First Republic (Malolo Republic)
  • Signed the Pact of Biak-na-Bato
  • Replacement of Spanish friars by Filipino priests
  • Created a truce between the Spanish and Philippine revolutionaries known as the President of the Revolutionary Government
Former President of the Philippines
Andrés Bonifacio
  • Born: November 30, 1863
  • Died: May 10, 1897 (33 yrs old)
  • Founder of the Katipunan
  • Led the rebellion against Spain
  • Replacement of Spanish friars by Filipino priests
  • Declared Philippine independence in 1896
A Filipino Revolutionary
Marcelo H. del Pilar
  • Born: August 30, 1850
  • Died: July 4, 1896 (45 yrs old)
  • Founded the Diariong Tagalog
  • Exposed the atrocities committed by the Spanish friars against the Filipinos
 A Filipino politician
Gabriela Silang
  • Born: March 19, 1731
  • Died: September 20, 1763 (32 yrs old)
  • Rallied Filipino forces to carry on with the war against Spain
  • Fought for Filipino independence
 A Filipino Revolutionary
